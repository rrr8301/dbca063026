  [Root cause classification: nextest missing + filesystem permission behavior difference in Docker]
  - The LLM generated a run.sh using cargo nextest but also appended redundant bare cargo build/nextest runs without the .cargo/config-ci.toml config, which caused the nexttest result to not be picked up by the harness parser (hence "nexttest not present" in LLM stats). The GoDefault entries are spurious lines from jj's CLI output being misinterpreted by the test parser during the build steps.
